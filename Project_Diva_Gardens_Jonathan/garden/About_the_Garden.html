About the Garden

Each of these fourteen beds, cultivated from former cow pastures, was installed to hold the overflow of cultivars acquired from various auctions and garden sales. While the outer beds are primarily for displaying new additions, the heart of our sales can be found just within the shade of our pecan trees. Our flowers have traits such as double blooms, bi-colors, unusual forms, and even some with teeth. On a fruitful peak blooming season, Diva Gardens bursts with a vibrant palate across many forms of canvas.
