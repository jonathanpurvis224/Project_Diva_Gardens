About the Diva

Starting with the propagation of cultivars from her Aunt Eleanor Janelle’s collection, Andrea Purvis was only beginning to amass the expansive garden today. After purchasing various daylilies from growers, such as Ruth Killingsworth, Bell’s Daylily Garden, and Canoochee Daylily Garden, The Daylily Diva decided to start her marketing business as a daylily farmer.

If you are interested in starting your own garden be sur to visit the Official Daylily Diva Facebook page!
