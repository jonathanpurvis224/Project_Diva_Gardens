FAQ

What does the price on the plant mean?

The price displayed is a unit price for two fans of a cultivar. If you wish to buy a six fan clump the price will be three times the unit price.

When is the best time to plant daylilies?

Autumn is the ideal season, as it does not stress the plant during bloom season, and the roots will be situated into the soil before the winter season.

Do you apply free shipping?

Yes, if your mail order is above $100 we will pay for the shipping; giving you more of an opportunity to invest in your garden.
